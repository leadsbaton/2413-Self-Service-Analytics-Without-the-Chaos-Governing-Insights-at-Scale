Self-Service Analytics Without the Chaos: Governing Insights at Scale

Self-service analytics promises faster answers, yet many organizations end up with conflicting dashboards, duplicated data, and renewed reliance on IT. The issue is not control versus flexibility, but delivering governed, analytics-ready data that supports both independent exploration and consistent reporting.

Recent studies show only 26% of employees say they fully trust the data they use for decisions. As access expands, inconsistent metrics and manual reconciliation erode confidence. The challenge shifts from enabling access to ensuring shared definitions, transparency, and accountability across the business.
